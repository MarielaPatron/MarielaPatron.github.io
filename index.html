Mariela Patron

Multimedia Journalist

I like to code and use data graphics, video and writing to report.
I have covered politics, education, business, labor and citizen activism.
I previously worked with the video team at The Wall Street Journal and at MSNBC as an NBC News Group Summer Fellow.
I graduated from the University of California Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism in 2017, and
I graduated summa cum laude from the University of La Verne with a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism in 2015.
Through multimedia platforms, I want to expand news consumption in the English and Spanish markets.
I am a first generation Mexican American, Los Angeles native and an NAHJ member.
